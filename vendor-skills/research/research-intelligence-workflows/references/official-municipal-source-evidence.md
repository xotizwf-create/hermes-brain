# Official municipal/source-backed evidence notes

Use this when the user needs an official source for a narrow factual claim (e.g., utility infrastructure, municipal systems, permits, regulations).

## Pattern

1. Prefer official domains first: city/administration sites, official gazettes, procurement portals, operator pages, regional/federal registries.
2. Search both the user's exact wording and adjacent technical terms. For Russian municipal infrastructure, useful variants include:
   - `"Схема водоснабжения и водоотведения" <город>`
   - `"общесплав" <город> канализация`
   - `"ливневые и талые воды" "канализационная сеть" <город>`
   - `"ливневая канализация" <город> "отсутствует"`
3. If an official archive contains DOCX/PDF attachments, download/extract them and search inside. ZIP/DOCX/PDF attachments often contain the authoritative text even when the HTML page is sparse.
4. Quote exact passages and include document title, issuing body, date/number, attachment/book/page if available, and direct URL.
5. Separate direct evidence from interpretation. If the document does not literally say the requested term, say: “the document does not use the term X, but it states Y and Z, which supports/converges with X.” Do not present an inferred label as a verbatim official statement.

## Example: Sarapul sewerage claim

Official source found during a session:

- Administration of Sarapul official site archive: `http://adm-sarapul.ru/city/zkh/post-3262-281224.zip`
- Document: Постановление Администрации города Сарапула №3262 от 28.12.2024, утверждающее «Схему водоснабжения и водоотведения МО "Город Сарапул" Удмуртской Республики на период 2025–2035 гг.»
- Relevant evidence in Book 2 included statements that the city lacks centralized storm sewerage, stormwater is discharged to relief without treatment, and during spring flood/rain part of storm runoff enters the city sewerage system; a wastewater balance separately counted “ливневые и талые воды, поступившие в канализационную сеть”.

Preferred answer style for this case: send the official URL first, then concise bullets with quoted evidence and page/book references, and avoid overstating that the document literally calls the system “общесплавная” unless that exact term is present.
